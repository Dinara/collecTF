CollecTF welcomes and encourages contributions

  • Suggest an article for curation.

    Know of a peer-reviewed publication describing experimentally-validated bacterial TF-binding sites that is not yet on our to-do list, Please let us know! Submit a publication for curation.

  • Submit data

    Are you the author of a publication with experimental data on bacterial TF-binding sites that is not already in our database? Please take a moment to submit your data and maximize its visibility and accessibility through NCBI RefSeq complete genome records.

    For your convenience, you may:

    • Submit data using our online submission system. Please check the accompanying submission guide before submitting. You must be registered as an external user to submit.
    • Submit data in table format. Please check the data submission guide before submitting. You must be registered as an external user to submit.
    • Submitted records will be assessed by CollecTF staff and incorporated into the database prior to submission to NCBI.

    • Become a curator interested in TF-binding sites? Intrigued by the intricacies of database curation? CollecTF is open to everyone willing and able to read primary scientific literature and extract relevant content. Please contact us if you want to become a member of the CollecTF team.
